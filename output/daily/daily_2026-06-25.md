### 04:00 Securing Time Integrity in Energy IoT Against Clock Drift and Y2K38 Failures

- **자료유형:** 논문
- **출처:** arXiv CS.AI
- **저자/발행기관:** arXiv CS.AI
- **발행일:** 2026-06-25
- **링크/DOI:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.23147
- **요약 (영문 원문):**
  - Ensuring time integrity is crucial for reliable operation in energy cyber-physical systems, such as smart grids and microgrids. However, IoT systems are vulnerable to clock drift, time-synchronization manipulation, and timestamp discontinuities.
  - Researchers introduce STGAT, a framework that models both temporal distortion and inter-device consistency in energy IoT systems. STGAT combines drift-aware temporal embeddings and temporal self-attention to capture corrupted time evolution at individual devices.
  - STGAT achieves 95.7% accuracy, outperforming recurrent, transformer, and graph-based baselines with significant improvements. Additionally, STGAT reduces detection delay by 26%, achieving a 2.3-time-step delay while maintaining stable performance under overflow, drift, and physical inconsistencies.
- **요약 (한국어 번역):**
  - 전력계통 및 미세 전력망과 같은 에너지 사이버 물리 시스템에서 시간의성이 신뢰할 수 있는 운영을 보장하는 데 중요함. 그러나 IoT 시스템은 시계, 시간 동기화 조작 및 타임스탬프 불연속성과 같은 위협에 취약함.
  - 연구자들은 STGAT이라는 프레임워크를 제안하여 에너지 IoT 시스템에서 시간 왜곡과 장치 간 일관성을 모델링함. STGAT은 시계에 대한 인지 temporal 임베딩 및 temporal self-attention을 결합하여 개별 장치의 시간 진화에 대한 손상된 시간 진화를 캡처함.
  - STGAT은 95.7%의 정확도를 달성하여 반복, 변환기 및 그래프 기반 기본 모델을 크게 개선함. 또한 STGAT은 감소된 감지 지연을 달성하여 26%의 감지 지연을 감소시키며, 오버플로우, 및 물리적 불일치하에서 안정적인 성능을 유지함.

- **요약 (영문 원문):**
  - The accelerated digitalization of smart grids through IoT sensors and SCADA systems has expanded the attack surface for cyberattacks, making real-time intrusion detection crucial. This study presents a hybrid CNN-LSTM framework for detecting instantaneous volumetric anomalies and evolving low and slow-attack campaigns.
  - The proposed framework jointly exploits CNN-based spatial feature extraction and LSTM-based temporal sequence modeling, enabling real-time detection of complex attacks. The model was trained using a seven-step preprocessing workflow and achieved high precision rates in benchmark datasets.
  - The framework's ability to detect instantaneous and evolving attacks in real-time, combined with its high precision rates and scalability, makes it a promising solution for securing next-generation smart grid infrastructure.

### 04:00 Supervised Reinforcement Learning for the Coordination of Distributed Energy Resources

- **자료유형:** 논문
- **출처:** arXiv CS.LG
- **저자/발행기관:** arXiv CS.LG
- **발행일:** 2026-06-25
- **링크/DOI:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.24947
- **요약 (영문 원문):**
  - The increasing integration of distributed energy resources (DERs) is crucial for power system decarbonization, but their inherent uncertainties and complexity hinder their flexibility. This paper proposes a Supervised Reinforcement Learning (SRL) framework to address this challenge.
  - The SRL framework pre-trains a policy on demonstration data in a supervised-learning fashion and then fine-tunes it using reinforcement learning. It also proposes a two-step fine-tuning process: offline fine-tuning for enhancing policy performance and online fine-tuning for adapting it to real-world dynamics.
  - The proposed framework significantly outperforms all benchmarks, achieving high cost efficiency even under low-quality demonstration data. This is a promising alternative to traditional optimization methods for DER management.
- **요약 (한국어 번역):**

- **요약 (영문 원문):**
  - This research proposes a framework for grid-friendly operation of industrial microgrids, focusing on tie-line power shaping. The framework uses multi-agent reinforcement learning to coordinate heterogeneous resources and achieve efficient and safe operation.
  - The proposed framework, called SHAC, models process loads, hydrogen storage, and battery storage as functionally heterogeneous agents.
