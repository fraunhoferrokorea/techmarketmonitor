### 04:00 Inference as Flexibility: Ramp Management for Transmission-Connected AI Data Centres

- **자료유형:** 논문
- **출처:** arXiv EESS.SY
- **저자/발행기관:** arXiv EESS.SY
- **발행일:** 2026-06-23
- **링크/DOI:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.21833
- **요약 (영문 원문):**
  - The rapid growth of large AI data centers introduces new operational challenges for power systems, including rapid ramping, oscillatory load behavior, voltage fluctuations, and supply-demand balancing impacts.
  - This paper presents a proof-of-concept demonstration of a complementary software-defined mitigation layer using flexible large language model (LLM) inference serving as endogenous flexibility to partially offset AI training power ramps.
  - The proposed approach uses LLM inference as a flexible resource to mitigate the impact of AI training power ramps on the power system, rather than relying on exogenous electrical resources such as battery energy storage systems (BESS).
- **요약 (한국어 번역):**
  - AI 데이터 센터의 급격한 성장으로 전력계통에 새로운 운영 과제가 발생하고 있으며, 급격한 람핑, 오실레이터 적재 거동, 전압 불안정, 공급-수요 균형 영향 등이 포함됨.
  - 이 논문은 소프트웨어 정의의 완충 계층으로서, 유연한 대규모 언어 모델(LLM) 추론을 사용하여 AI 교육용 전력 램프를 부분적으로 보상하는 내재된 TCDC 유연성을 제공하는 증명적 개념을 제시함.
  - 제안된 접근 방식은 AI 교육용 전력 램프의 영향을 완화하기 위해 LLM 추론을 유연한 자원으로 사용하는 대신, 전기적 자원인 배터리 에너지 저장 시스템(BESS)과 같은 외래 전기 자원을 의존하지 않음.

- **요약 (영문 원문):**
  - The Power Systems Agent Benchmark is a new tool for evaluating AI agents in electric power engineering. This benchmark aims to assess the performance of AI agents in real-world power engineering tasks.
  - The benchmark consists of 41 task families across eight areas of power engineering, including power flow, protection, stability, microgrids, reliability, power quality, and forecasting.   - The Power Systems Agent Benchmark is the first executable benchmark for power-engineering agents, allowing for a more comprehensive evaluation of AI agents in this field. Unlike language-model use, which is still dominated by retrieval and text question answering, this benchmark assesses agents acting on power-system artifacts.
